A great ebook about System design in Japanese

2020 July 5th

As my current task at work is investigating and analyzing customer's system, I have to do some researching about system analysis and stuff. My current customer has more than 30 systems running around, most of them don't have documents/documents are not updated for years.

And then I came across this ebook: システム再構築を成功に導くユーザガイド (User guide for successful System rebuilding). It's written by Software Reliability Enhancement Center of Information-technology Promotion Agency, Japan (or IPA in short). The second version of the book was released on February 2nd 2018 and available for free in PDF.

This book is aimed at companies who have legacy system, and due to the rapidly changing business environment, they need to rebuild a new system.

There are 4 parts in the book, I will try to translate them into English :
  • 解説編 (Explanation): When and why we need to build a new system etc
  • 再構築手法選択編 (How to choose a system rebuild method): Steps to investigate, analyze current system and pick out the rebuild method.
  • 計画策定編 (Planning): Explain some viewpoints to consider when planning for system rebuild
  • 事例編 (Case study)
I don't read many IT Japanese books, but I think this is a good book for anyone who is working with system design, analysis etc., especially in Japanese company.
